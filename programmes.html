Key Programmes

Driving Sustainable Change Across Health, Livelihood, and Environment.

Women Self Help Groups (SHGs)

Since 2010, MGD has focused on economic empowerment through SHGs, facilitating loans for sustainable livelihood activities.

Facilitating NABFIN loans for flour mills, animal husbandry, and schools. Includes training on group rules and finances.

Water & Sanitation (WASH)

Implementing government water and sanitation programs across multiple districts.

Focus Areas:

  • Water Management: Training on quality, maintenance, and harvesting.
  • Sanitation: Economic latrines and waste-to-fertilizer models.
  • Community Hygiene: 'Plastic-free villages' and tribal sewage planning.

Health & Agriculture

Running large-scale projects for public health and sustainable agriculture.

Major Initiatives:

  • Health Camps: 74,000+ patients, 5,000+ surgeries since 2022.
  • Silt-Free Dam: Enhancing water storage and soil fertility.
  • Shankar Pat: Agricultural fairs for community engagement.

Skill Training

Equipping women with certified vocational skills for livelihoods.

  • Vocational: Certified stitching and beauty parlor courses.
  • Watershed: Recognized agriculture training.
  • Awareness: AIDS programs and tree plantation.

पाणी आणि स्वच्छता (WASH)

पाणी आणि स्वच्छतेसाठी शासकीय योजनांची अंमलबजावणी.

  • पाणी: गुणवत्ता, देखभाल आणि संधारणाचे प्रशिक्षण.
  • स्वच्छता: आर्थिक शौचालये आणि कचरा व्यवस्थापन.
  • आरोग्य: 'प्लॅस्टिकमुक्त गावे' आणि सांडपाणी नियोजन.

आरोग्य आणि कृषी

सार्वजनिक आरोग्य आणि शाश्वत शेती सुधारणा.

  • आरोग्य शिबिरे: ७४,०००+ रुग्ण, ५,०००+ शस्त्रक्रिया.
  • गाळमुक्त धरण: पाणी साठा आणि मातीची सुपीकता वाढ.
  • शंकर पाट: कृषी मेळावे.

कौशल्य विकास

उपजीविकेसाठी व्यावसायिक कौशल्ये प्रदान करणे.

  • प्रशिक्षण: शिवणकाम आणि ब्युटी पार्लर अभ्यासक्रम.
  • जलग्रहण: कृषी विभागाद्वारे मान्यताप्राप्त प्रशिक्षण.
  • जागरूकता: एड्स कार्यक्रम आणि वृक्षारोपण.
